Welcome to mOS

An Ubuntu-based Distro by 2 Students

About mOS

mOS is a modern and lightweight Linux distribution built on top of Ubuntu. It is developed by two passionate students who aim to provide an intuitive and user-friendly operating system.

Key Features

  • Fast and responsive performance
  • Minimalistic and modern user interface
  • Optimized for productivity and ease of use
  • Built-in support for popular development tools
  • Secure and reliable

System Requirements

  • Processor: 2 GHz dual-core
  • Memory: 2 GB RAM
  • Storage: 20 GB available space
  • Graphics: 1024x768 resolution
